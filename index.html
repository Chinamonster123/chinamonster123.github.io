  • 2019.12 - 2020.6
    Graduation Thesis
    solo
    This article first analyzes the principles of deep neural networks, and then analyzes the basic principles of YOLOV3 and Mask RCNN network structure. In the experimental part, the YOLOv3 algorithm is selected as the main object detection algorithm model for road car detection. In addition, the Mask RCNN model is used as a supplement to realize the instance segmentation of road cars. Take road cars as the target of instance segmentation, select 2536 sheets from the public data set DETRAC as the training data set, and 500 sheets as the verification data set. Through the construction of the target detection model, the training of the data set, the result test and optimization four The module completes the realization of YOLOv3 and Mask-RCNN. The experimental results show that the optimized YOLOv3 model can achieve 92.22% mAP (average accuracy) on the DETRAC data set, and the number of video running frames has reached 10FPS. The Mask-RCNN model also achieves the task of instance segmentation for road car pictures. It basically meets the requirements for real-time target detection of road cars, and proves the feasibility of YOLOv3 and Mask-RCNN for real-time target detection of road cars.
